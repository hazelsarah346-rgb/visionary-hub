# Visionary Hub — Problem & Solution (in-app)

This doc matches the problem we solve to the value we show in the app. Use it for copy, pitch, and product decisions.

---

## Who we serve

- **Students** — in school, deciding direction, building toward first steps
- **Career switchers** — changing industry, role, or path
- **Figuring it out** — not sure what they want, feeling stuck or unclear

---

## The problem

- **No clear north star** — goals are vague or only job-focused; hard to make consistent decisions
- **Going it alone** — no structured way to reflect, get feedback, or stay accountable
- **Mismatched advice** — generic tips don’t fit their situation or stage
- **Overwhelm** — too many options; hard to see a concrete path from here to there

**One line:** They want to move forward but lack clarity, structure, and support that fits their stage.

---

## How we solve it (in the app)

| Problem | In-app solution |
|--------|------------------|
| No clear north star | **Vision Canvas** — big vision, purpose, strengths, obstacle, 12‑month goal in one place |
| Going it alone | **Flow** (share & support) + **Find Peers** + **Mentorship** — community and accountability |
| Mismatched advice | **AI Tutor, Roadmap, Insights** — personalized to their canvas and journal |
| Overwhelm | **Life Roadmap** + **Opportunities** — concrete steps and next actions |
| No reflection habit | **Reflect** — journal + AI insights for real-life clarity |

---

## Value we add (messaging in the app)

1. **Clarity** — One place to define where you’re going and why.
2. **Structure** — Canvas → roadmap → opportunities + journal so effort is focused.
3. **Support that fits** — Peers and mentors who get “figuring it out”; AI that uses their context.
4. **Momentum** — Daily insight, accountability, and a feed that supports progress.
5. **No more alone** — “You don’t have to figure it out alone.”

---

## Where this shows up in the app

- **Auth:** “Clarity, structure & support — so you don’t have to figure it out alone”; signup: “For students, career switchers & anyone figuring it out…”
- **Sidebar (NAV):** Each tab’s subtitle states the value (e.g. “Share & support — you’re not alone”, “Your north star & 12‑month goal”).
- **Flow:** Header “No more figuring it out alone”; canvas nudge “Get clarity… then AI, roadmap & mentors work for you”; empty state “your community is here so you don’t have to figure it out alone”.
- **Product name:** Visionary **Hub** (consistent across auth, sidebar, 2FA).

Use this framing for any new screens, empty states, or marketing so the app clearly presents itself as the desirable solution to this problem.
